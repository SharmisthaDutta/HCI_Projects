Open-ended Questions

Adult: what did you like the least about the voice-enabled application?

Not enough images, "hate the colors", "rhinoceros" not easily recognized

Adult: do you have any other comments, feature requests, or suggestions?

Would prefer iOS app

Summary

For adult users accustomed to Android's material design guidelines (and iOS applications), the adult mode navigation was exceedingly simple. Also, adults instantly learned how to identify illustrations and move to new illustrations from within child mode. A more diverse (e.g. older adults, minimally experienced phone users) test user distribution and a larger test sample size is needed for determining the success of the UI, but the target demographic, young adults with children, were quick to adapt to the user interface and implemented widgets.
