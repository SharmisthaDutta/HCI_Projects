Guideline:

Depending on the privileges the navigation areas of the multi user app may be customizable For example admin users might have some more menu options than normal users. The app should display navigartion areas without hiding the shared document. It will be problematic to users of app otherwise.

Comments:

though our studies we found that displaying the navigation areas is very important for a good multi-user app. However upon clicking the navigation areas it should not interfere with the shared content.

Sources:

Multi user apps that we studied.

Good Example:

Teamup, Skype: navigation areas does not interfere with shared content.

Bad Example:

Google Docs: Upon click on a menu option from top, interferes with part of the shared document.
