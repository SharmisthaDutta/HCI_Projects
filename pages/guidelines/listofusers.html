Guideline:

Provide a exclusive list to users to that they can easily locate other users currently viewing/hearing the shared content

Comments:

Providing list of users helps. The Attendee/user List provides organizers/presenters with a list of all shared content users and enables them to make other users controller of shared content( if applicable) grant or revoke users privileges and dismiss users (if applicable).

Sources:

All of the multiusers app that we studied.

Good Example:

Skype: Explicitely shows the list of users. Very intuitive and informative.

Bad Example:

Google docs: Shows color icons against each users of shared content and you have to click on them to see the users.
