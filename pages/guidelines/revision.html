Guideline:

It is recommended by several users to show revision history of shared object where applicable and where the object is visible and include the time of editing the shared object and object owner. as this helps to resolve any conflicts that might come along with the collaboration.

Comments:

We personally came accross a user who used an multi user app for her course. It was a team project documentation and unfortunalety it let to a conflict amongst team members due to an issue of plagiarism. The app helped her to resolve the problem and prove her not guilty of plagiarism. So it was very helpful.
