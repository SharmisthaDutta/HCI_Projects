Guideline:

Use black text on plain, high contrast and non-patterned background on shared object.
Avoid colors that are hard for color blind people to recognize.E.g: dark red vs black,
dark red vs dark green, blue vs purple.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Comments:

Be careful about selection of colors for collaboration. Simultaneous collaboration color helps
However take into account all sorts of users.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Sources:

Multi user apps that we studied.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Good Example:

Dropbox: Nice colors.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Bad Example:

Cloud 9, Google docs, Teamup: When there are a good amount of users/collaborators (>6 people) the colors those app
choose for collaboration are not very suitable for color-blind people.
